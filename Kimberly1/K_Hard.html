Recipe: Pumpkin Clam Chowder

Price: Time:
$ 15-20 mins

Rate the dish for cost & time!




Image Source

Ingredients:

  • 6 bacon strips chopped
  • 1 medium fennel bulb, chopped, fronds reserved
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/2 cup chardonnay or clam juice
  • 5 bottles (8oz each) of clam juice divided
  • 4 cups of cut 1/2 inch cube of either:
    • Fresh pumpkin
    • Butternet squash
  • 2 medium potatos pelled and cut into small 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cans (10oz each) whole baby clams, drained
  • 1-1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • Either:
    • 1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon
    • 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Instructions:

  1. In an oven, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp
  2. Remove and place bacon on a paper towel to rest
  3. Cook and stir chopped fennel and onion in the bacon drippings over medium heat till tender (8-10 mins)
  4. Add chardonnay to pan and increase to medium-high heat
  5. Cook 1-2 minutes while stiring
  6. Add 4-1/2 cups of clam juice, the pumpkin and potatoes
  7. Once it comes to a boil, reduce the heat and let simmer with the lid uncovered until tender, stir occasionally (15-20 mins)
  8. Whisk flour and remaining 1/2 cup of clam juice and stir into pan
  9. Stir until boiling and until thickened (2-3 mins)
  10. Stir in clams, cream, tarragon, salt, and pepper
  11. Let simmer in heat (Do not allow to boil)
  12. Serve in bowl and top with fresh chopped fennel fronds and chopped bacon
  13. Dig in!

Recipe: Open-Faced Pizza Burgers

Price: Time:
$ 10 mins

Rate the dish for cost & time!




Image Source

Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 can (15 ounces) pizza sauce
  • 1 can (4 ounces) mushroom stems and pieces, drained
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 6 hamburger buns, split and toasted
  • 1-1/2 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large cast-iron or other heavy skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink, 3-5 minutes, breaking into crumbles
  2. Drain. Stir in the pizza sauce, mushrooms, sugar and oregano; mix well
  3. Spoon onto buns; sprinkle with mozzarella cheese
  4. Place on ungreased baking sheets. Broil 4 in. from the heat until cheese is melted, 2 minutes
  5. Freeze option: Place the split and toasted buns on a baking sheet. Spoon the meat mixture onto buns; freeze for 1 hour. Transfer to freezer-safe airtight containers
  6. Dig in!

Recipe: Grilled Steak Bruschetta Salad

Price: Time:
$ 10-15 mins

Rate the dish for cost & time!




Image Source

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound beef tenderloin steaks (1 inch thick)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 slices Italian bread (1/2 inch thick)
  • 1 cup of either:
    • Fresh arugula
    • Fresh baby spinach
  • 1/3 cup jarred or prepared bruschetta topping
  • 1/3 cup blue cheese salad dressing

Instructions:

  1. Sprinkle steaks with salt and pepper
  2. Grill, covered, over medium heat until meat reaches desired doneness (for medium-rare, a thermometer should read 135°; medium, 140°; medium-well, 145°), 6-8 minutes on each side
  3. Let stand for 5 minutes
  4. Grill bread, covered, until toasted, 1-2 minutes on each side; place on salad plates
  5. Thinly slice steak; arrange over toast
  6. Top with arugula and bruschetta topping. Drizzle with dressing
  7. Dig in!
