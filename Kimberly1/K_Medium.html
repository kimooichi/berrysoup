Recipe: Air-fried Breaded Shrimp

Price: Time:
$ 15-20 mins

Rate the dish for cost & time!




Image Source

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon seafood seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon dill weed
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 pound uncooked shrimp, peeled
  • Cooking spray
  • (Optional)
    • Lemon wedges
    • Seafood cocktail sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat air fryer to 375 degrees
  2. mix flour, seasoned salt, dill weed and pepper in a bowl
  3. In a seperate bowl, whisk the egg, milk, and hot pepper sauce until combined
  4. Place bread crumbs into another bowl
  5. Dip and coat shrimp fully in flour mixture and then shake off excess
  6. Dip shrimp itno egg mixture and then bread crumbs
  7. Pat bread crumbs into shrimp
  8. Place shrimps in a single layer in the air fryer after spraying coooking spray
  9. Let rest until golden brown (3-4 minutes for each side)
  10. Remove and let cool on a plate then dig in!

Recipe: Quick Chili

Price: Time:
$ 10 mins

Rate the dish for cost & time!




Image Source

Ingredients:

  • 1 large onion
  • 1/4 cup egg beaters
  • 1/2 cup ctushed Fiber One cereal or bread crumbs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Cut edges off onion and remove outer layer
    • Cut into 1/2 inch slices and separate into rings
  3. Fill a small bowl with egg beaters
  4. Using a blender, blend cereal until a crumb consistency and pour onto a small dish
  5. Dip each ring into egg and coat with cereal or crumbs and place on a baking sheet with nonstick spray
  6. Cook for 20-25 minutes flipping rings half way
  7. Dig in!

Recipe: Couscous

Price: Time:
$ 10-15 mins

Rate the dish for cost & time!




Image Source

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Couscous
  • 1 cup of frozen peas and corn
  • 1/2 cup of water or vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional:
    • Dash of hot sauce
    • Sliced almonds as garnish

Instructions:

  1. Bring water or stock to boil in a saucepan. Stir in couscous and peas and carrots
  2. Add salt and remove from heat
  3. Let sit for 5 minutes then stir in black pepper and hot sauce
  4. Dig in!
