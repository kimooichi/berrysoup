FAQ

Are these wallpapers free to use?

Yes! All my wallpapers are free to use and download on any devices.


Can I use these art pieces for my own personal uses?

Any use that discredits my art, commercialize, and gain profit without my knowledge is respectfully not allowed. For any inquiries, please contact beforehand :)


What devices can I use these wallpapers for?

My "Desktop" Wallpapers work for laptops, pc, and tablets while my "Mobile" Wallpapers work for phones.


Where can I find your art?

Currently, my art is only circulating within Instagram, Pinterest, and this website.


Do you only make wallpapers?

Right now I only make wallpapers but I hope to venture out into other projects in the future :)


Why did you choose to make these wallpapers and share them for free?

Realistically, I needed a way to practice both art and coding. I've always enjoyed making art but often fell into art blocks. Centering all my wallpapers around this imaginary character "Pippo" that i've created, allows me to create art without having to think too much about the concept. At the same time, I wanted to draw things that brought me comfort and with so many people encouraging me to release these designs, I gave in :)
